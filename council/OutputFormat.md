# Output Format

## Quick Council Output

```markdown
## Quick Council: [Topic]

### Perspectives

**[Persona 1]:**
[Short take]

**[Persona 2]:**
[Short take]

**[Persona 3]:**
[Short take]

### Summary
- **Consensus:** [what they broadly agree on]
- **Biggest concern:** [main red flag]
- **Recommendation:** [proceed / revise / stop / investigate]
- **Need full council?:** [yes/no + why]
```

## Full Council Output

```markdown
## Council Debate: [Topic]

### Round 1 — Initial Positions
**[Persona]:**
[Position]

### Round 2 — Responses & Challenges
**[Persona]:**
[Direct responses to others]

### Round 3 — Final Positions
**[Persona]:**
[Final stance, convergence, disagreement]

### Synthesis
- **Areas of convergence:**
  - [...]
- **Remaining disagreements:**
  - [...]
- **Recommended path:**
  - [...]
- **Key tradeoffs:**
  - [...]
- **Open questions:**
  - [...]
- **Next action:**
  - [...]
```

## Length Guidance

### Quick Council
- 40-80 words per persona
- brief synthesis

### Full Council
- Round 1: 80-150 words per persona
- Round 2: 80-150 words per persona
- Round 3: 60-120 words per persona
- synthesis: concise but decisive

## Hard Requirements

- Round 2+ must reference specific persona points.
- Do not let personas repeat their Round 1 answer with fresh wallpaper.
- The synthesis must name a recommendation.
- If information is insufficient, say what is missing.
- Keep the final answer useful for decision-making, not theatre.
